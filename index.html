SHIPPING ANNOUNCEMENT:

As we strive to reduce plastic waste and contribute to a healthier planet, I'm committed to doing my part. In these challenging times of increased online shopping, I've accumulated a stack of boxes and envelopes that I don't want to throw away because they can be reused. That's why I've decided to repurpose them!

Please note that your order may arrive in recycled packaging, but the inside will be wrapped in new tissue or craft paper.

If you're ordering a gift or want your package to look brand new, simply select the gift option or send me a message, and I'll make sure it's wrapped accordingly.

Thank you for your support in reducing waste and preserving our planet!
